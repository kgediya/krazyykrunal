Privacy Policy

SkyHi is a multi-surface spatial messaging experience that lets people send and view lantern messages across a shared sky. This page explains what information SkyHi may process when you use the web companion, immersive receiver, notifications, and optional Snapchat login.

Effective date: April 2, 2026
Contact: kgediya0898@gmail.com

Information SkyHi Uses

When you use SkyHi, the experience may process the message content you send, the Sky PIN you choose or join, the display name you enter, and basic technical data required to deliver the service across devices.

  • Lantern messages you type and send into a Sky PIN.
  • Display names you provide in the web companion.
  • Sky PINs, saved skies, sent-message history, and audio or interface preferences stored locally in your browser.
  • Push notification subscription details if you enable browser notifications for a Sky PIN.
  • Optional Snapchat OAuth information if you choose to log in with Snapchat, such as authentication tokens and approved identity fields.

How Information Is Used

SkyHi uses information only to operate the messaging experience, connect shared skies across surfaces, and support optional login and notification features.

  • To send, fetch, and display lantern messages in the shared sky.
  • To personalize the sender experience with a name or approved Snapchat identity.
  • To remember local preferences and saved Sky PINs on your device.
  • To send browser notifications if you explicitly enable them.
  • To maintain service reliability, security, and abuse protection.

Snapchat Login

SkyHi may offer "Log in with Snapchat" using Snap Login Kit through OAuth 2.0. This login is optional.

  • SkyHi only requests the scopes needed for account sign-in and approved identity display.
  • Depending on the permissions you grant, SkyHi may receive fields such as display name, app-specific external ID, or Bitmoji avatar.
  • SkyHi does not use Snap Login to access private Snapchat messages, contacts, or personal content.
  • If you disconnect or revoke access, SkyHi will stop using the active Snapchat session for that browser.

Storage and Third Parties

SkyHi uses browser storage on your device for convenience features and may use backend infrastructure to deliver shared sky messages.

  • Local browser storage may keep saved Sky PINs, sent history, notification state, and optional login session data on your device.
  • SkyHi uses Supabase-backed infrastructure and edge functions to route shared sky message data.
  • Browser notifications depend on your browser vendor and push service provider.
  • Snapchat login depends on Snap's OAuth services when you choose to use that feature.

Your Choices

You can control how much information SkyHi uses by choosing which features you enable.

  • You can use SkyHi without logging in with Snapchat.
  • You can clear local browser data for this site to remove saved skies, sent history, and locally stored session state.
  • You can disable browser notifications at any time in the app or your browser settings.
  • You can avoid sending sensitive information in lantern messages.

Security and Children

SkyHi is designed with reasonable measures to protect service data, but no internet transmission or storage method is completely secure.

  • Please do not share sensitive personal, financial, or highly confidential data through SkyHi messages.
  • SkyHi is not intended for children to use without appropriate supervision where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Overview

A concise walkthrough of the end-to-end experience

Here is the simplest way to understand the product from first interaction to cross-device use.

SkyHi introduces a spatial messaging format.

A message becomes a lantern inside a shared room. It can be created, sent, discovered, and revisited instead of disappearing into a flat thread.

On Spectacles, a user creates a lantern through voice and gesture.

The sender pinches and speaks into a lantern, then uses directional drag gestures to send it, discard it, or switch its form before delivery.

The receiver experiences messages as a spatial inbox.

Messages appear as clustered unread lanterns in front of the user and migrate outward when read, so the environment itself communicates state and priority.

The same room model extends across companion surfaces.

A web companion already supports sending, immersive receiving, saved skies, and notification hooks. A watch-lite companion adds a lightweight surface for quick send and inbox refresh.
